              Our Mission

              Our mission is to foster independence and integration for individuals with disabilities by providing them with highly trained assistance dogs and ongoing aftercare.

              About Dogs with Wings Assistance Dog Society

              We offer four programs to serve our community: Service Dogs for youth and adults with physical disabilities or mobility issues; Autism Service Dogs for children with Autism Spectrum Disorder; Facility Dogs for professionals such as teachers, psychologists, agencies and courtrooms; and Companion Dogs for service provision within the home.

              Dogs with Wings provides assistance dogs at a cost of $1.00 to the client.  All costs of breeding, raising, fostering, training, placing and providing ongoing aftercare is covered by donations and fundraising activities. Dogs with Wings receives no sustainable government funding.

              Dogs with Wings operates with a small staff and relies on hundreds of volunteers who provide more than 100,000 volunteer hours per year.
